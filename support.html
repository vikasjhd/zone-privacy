Frequently Asked Questions

Why does Zone need Screen Time permissions?

Zone uses Apple's native Family Controls and DeviceActivity APIs to physically prevent you from opening distracting apps while a focus session is active. This is required for the app shielding feature to work natively on iOS.

Can you see which apps I am blocking?

Absolutely not. Zone operates on a strict zero-knowledge architecture. Apple's frameworks handle the app selection and shielding completely locally on your device. Your data is never sent to any external servers.

How do I cancel a session if Strict Mode is on?

You can't. That is the point of Strict Mode. If you enable Strict Mode, the "End Session" button is disabled to prevent you from abandoning your deep work block. You must wait for the timer to complete to unlock your device's shielded apps.

Why isn't the Live Activity showing on my Dynamic Island?

Ensure that you are running iOS 16.2 or later, and check that Live Activities are enabled for Zone in your iPhone's main Settings app (Settings > Zone > Live Activities).

How does the Apple Health integration work?

If enabled in Settings, Zone can save your completed deep work sessions to the Apple Health app as "Mindful Minutes." We only request write access, meaning we cannot read your personal health metrics. If sessions are not syncing, check iOS Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices > Zone to ensure permission is granted.
